HELP & SAFETY

How It Works

1. Add Emergency Contacts
Add trusted contacts who will receive alerts if something goes wrong. Trial users can add 1 contact, Pro users get unlimited contacts.

2. Check-In Before Showings
Before entering a property, tap "Start Check-In". A 30-minute timer begins. You can extend time in 15-minute increments if needed.

3. Location Tracking
Your GPS location is automatically captured when you check in. This exact location (including street address) will be sent to your contacts if you trigger SOS.

4. Timer Alerts & Check-Out
When the timer expires, you'll be asked if you're safe. Tap "Check Out" when you leave safely. If you don't respond or indicate danger, SOS is triggered automatically.

Emergency SOS

Tap the red "EMERGENCY SOS" button at any time if you feel unsafe. This will instantly:

  • Send emergency emails to all your contacts with your exact location
  • Open SMS messages pre-filled with your location and emergency alert
  • Provide a direct link to call 911
  • Display your current address on screen for quick reference

Tips & Troubleshooting

Location not working? Allow location permissions when your browser prompts you.

Need more contacts? Upgrade to Pro for unlimited emergency contacts for just $3/month.
